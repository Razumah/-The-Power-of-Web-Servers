Executive Summary

The World Wide Web has transformed how we communicate and work. At its core, web servers are the unsung heroes powering this global connectivity.These powerful systems store, process, and deliver web content making it possible for users to access websites, stream media, shop online, and stay connected across the globe. From the moment you type a URL into your browser to the second a page loads, a web server is at work behind the scenes, responding to your request in milliseconds.
